## Judge Talking Points

- WhatsApp is already the workflow.
- Sah.Bukti does not auto-confirm payments.
- Review is the trust boundary.
- Approval is the only path that changes ledger truth.
- Export is the month-end payoff.

## What Was Built

- FastAPI backend with SQLite source of truth
- WhatsApp evidence intake and webhook flow
- Review queue for payment proofs
- Approval-driven ledger mutation
- Invoices, customers, and dashboard views
- Accountant export and month-end readiness
- Provision engine for doubtful debts under MFRS 9 wording
- React frontend served from FastAPI

## What Was Deliberately Not Claimed

- Not a full accounting suite
- Not a generic chatbot
- Not an ERP
- Not LHDN e-invoicing ready
- Not auto-payment confirmation
